# 📊 Superstore Advanced Sales & Profit Analysis (Power BI)

This project presents an advanced Business Intelligence dashboard built in **Power BI**, using the Superstore dataset to analyze **sales performance, profitability, and business trends**.

The goal is to go beyond basic reporting and deliver **actionable insights** that support strategic decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

This analysis was designed to answer key business questions:

- How is sales performance evolving over time?
- Which categories and sub-categories drive the most revenue and profit?
- Are there regions with strong sales but low profitability?
- How can we identify opportunities to improve margins?

---

# 📈 Key Metrics (KPIs)

The dashboard focuses on core business indicators:

- **Total Revenue**
- **Total Profit**
- **Profit Margin (%)**
- **Average Order Value**

These KPIs provide a high-level overview of both **growth and efficiency**.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

### 📅 Sales Trend Analysis
- Revenue evolution over time
- Identification of growth patterns and seasonality

### 🌎 Regional Performance
- Sales and profit by region
- Identification of high-performing and underperforming areas

### 📦 Product Analysis
- Performance by category and sub-category
- Detection of high-revenue but low-profit segments

### 💰 Profitability Insights
- Profit vs Revenue comparison
- Margin analysis across different dimensions

The project uses the **Superstore dataset**, a widely used dataset for sales and business analysis.

- Orders

- Profit

---

# 💡 Key Insights

Some of the main insights identified:

- Certain categories generate high revenue but low profit margins  
- Some regions perform well in sales but underperform in profitability  
- Profitability varies significantly across product segments  

These findings highlight opportunities for:
- Cost optimization  
- Pricing strategy improvements  
- Product portfolio adjustments
